Soft Cheesesteak Rolls

Ingredients
  • 159g Bread Flour
  • 97g Water
  • 1.6g Instant Dry Yeast (approx. 1/2 tsp)
  • 2.1g Salt
  • 7.1g Crisco (Vegetable Oil as a substitute)
  • 2.4g Diastatic Malt Powder (approx. 1 1/4 tsp)
  • 6.3g Scratch Dough Conditioner (approx. 1 Tbsp)
Instructions
  1. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl, stir until combined, cover, and let rest for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Knead dough for 3-5 minutes until smooth, then let it rise in the bowl until it grows in size by 25-50%, about 30-60 minutes.
  3. Divide dough into two equal pieces, form each into a ball, and place them into spacious containers. Cover and refrigerate for 12-48 hours. (For same-day baking, leave at room temperature).
  4. Bring dough to room temperature and ensure it has grown at least 2X in size.
  5. Shape each dough ball into a 9” roll: flatten into a 1/2” thick triangle, roll from the narrow end, and close off the seam.
  6. Place rolls on parchment sprinkled with cornmeal, cover with a damp towel, and let them rise.
  7. Preheat oven to 425F.
  8. Bake rolls when they have doubled in size and spring back slowly when poked: 12 minutes with steam, then 4-6 minutes without steam, rotating halfway.
  9. Let rolls cool (30-60 minutes) and place them in a plastic bag for at least 3-4 hours before cutting.
 Makes: 2 10” Rolls
